Open console

By setting up visual studio code, you have to click the three line on the top left in your screen. Then you click file and you find the fifth option and click on it. It will open your folders and you pick a folder that you want code in it.

The console screen helps you debug the code and also your javascript that is written in your html screen. You right click on the screen and pick the last option inspect and click on console on the top row.
